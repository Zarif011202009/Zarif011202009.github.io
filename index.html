Why did I choose to study CSE?
To answer this, we have to dive deep into my childhood, where I had spent most of my time with computers. My fascination for computers stayed with me up until I passed my HSC exam, when the fascination turned into a genuine interest and a career choice. I have always had interest in solving problems, churning out simple solutions for complex problems. Moreover, I have always looked up to people like Larry Page, Mark Zuckerberg, James Gosling and several other influential developers of the world. I aspire to develop programs that one day would hopefully impact people's lives in a positive manner and make the world a better place. Studying CSE will help me achieve this aspiration, which is why I chose to study CSE.
